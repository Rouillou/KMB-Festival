Cookie policy

This cookie policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our KMB Festival website. By using the site, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device (computer, cell phone, etc.) when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to enable the site to operate efficiently, improve the user experience, provide information to the site owners and perform various other functions.

Types of cookies used

Essential cookies: These cookies are necessary for the site to function properly. They enable us to provide basic functionalities such as page navigation, access to secure areas of the site and management of your preferences. Without these cookies, certain site functions may not function properly.

Performance cookies: These cookies collect anonymous information on how visitors use the site, such as pages visited, time spent on each page and any errors encountered. This information helps us to improve the site's performance and user-friendliness.

Functionality cookies: These cookies enable the site to remember your choices and provide enhanced, more personalized functionality. For example, they may be used to remember your language preferences or to provide you with content based on your location.

Cookie control

You can control and manage cookies in a number of ways.
Most web browsers allow you to configure your cookie preferences via the browser settings.
You can generally accept all cookies, reject all cookies, delete existing cookies or receive notification when a cookie is placed on your device.
Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain site functions.

Changes to the cookie policy

Any changes will be posted on this page with an indication of the date of the last update.
We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed of any updates.

Changes to the privacy policy
