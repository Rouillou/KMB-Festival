the kmb festival

KMB Festival is an event designed to promote the musical heritage and techno
scene while creating a unique cultural experience.
For several years, the team has been working on a festival that will bring
together the best in music and staging.

"This first edition is a trial by fire for my whole team and myself, we can't wait to
share this moment with you and we'll be there in every situation to let you live
this experience to the full!" Nicolas, KMB creator

KMB 2023: a 100% all-female line-up showcasing tomorrow's artists on the
techno scene.


















KMB commits

The team is planning a memorable weekend to raise awareness of the fight against breast cancer.

Breast cancer is the most common form of cancer in women, affecting almost 1 in 8 women in their lifetime.

On the occasion of Pink October, part of our profits will be donated to the Caen Breast Cancer League.




Practical information

We look forward to seeing you on October 6 and 7, 2023 at the Parc des Expositions de Caen, 21 rue
Joseph Philippon, 14000 CAEN.

You will receive a map and the rules and regulations when you buy your ticket.

Tickets and identity will be checked at the entrance. The festival is for adults only.
